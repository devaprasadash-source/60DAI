RTCFR

Role: You are a Senior Wholesale Credit Risk Officer at a Global Investment Bank responsible for conducting credit assessments of corporate counterparties.

Task: Your responsibilities are to:
	- Retrieve the 2024 Annual Reports for:
		Wipro Limited
		Infosys Limited
	- Access and review the publicly available PDF annual financial statements.
	- Extract and analyze key financial information from:
		Balance Sheet
		Profit & Loss Statement
		Income Statement
		Cash Flow Statement (if available)

Structure the extracted financial data into an Excel-compatible format, including:
	- Historical financial figures
	- Key financial ratios
	- Trend analysis

Produce a formal credit analysis report based on the extracted financial data.

Context:
Financial reviews are conducted at the beginning of each month as part of the bank’s credit monitoring process.
The analysis must assess the financial health and creditworthiness of the counterparty.

The analysis should focus on:
	Profitability
	Liquidity
	Leverage
	Cash flow generation
	Balance sheet strength
	Revenue and margin trends

The final output should include MS Excel-style structured dataset containing:

Sheet 1 – Financial Statements

Revenue
EBITDA
Net Profit
Total Assets
Total Liabilities
Equity
Cash & Cash Equivalents
Debt
Operating Cash Flow


Sheet 2 – Key Financial Ratios

EBITDA Margin
Net Profit Margin
Debt / Equity
Interest Coverage Ratio
Current Ratio
Return on Equity (ROE)
Return on Assets (ROA)


Sheet 3 – Trend Analysis
3–5 year financial trends
Growth rates
Margin evolution


2. Credit Review Report

Prepare a formal wholesale credit risk report structured as follows:
Executive Summary
Company Overview
Industry Overview
Financial Performance Analysis
Balance Sheet Strength
Liquidity and Cash Flow Analysis
Key Credit Risks
Credit Strengths
Financial Ratio Analysis
Credit Opinion

3. Credit Decision Section

Provide a sanctioning recommendation, including:
Recommended credit limit
Risk rating / credit grade
Key conditions or covenants
Final decision:
Approve
Approve with conditions
Decline

UI Requirement
Design a simple user interface concept that includes:
File upload option for annual reports
Editable financial fields
Auto-calculated financial ratios
Editable credit commentary sections
Export functionality to Excel and PDF

Few Shots:
Use examples consistent with wholesale credit risk reports produced by global investment banks, such as those used in corporate lending or counterparty credit assessments.
Each financial section should include analytical commentary explaining what the numbers imply for the counterparty’s creditworthiness.

Report/Tone:
Professional
Analytical
Concise but comprehensive
Suitable for internal bank credit committee review
Written to support credit decision-making and risk approval processes
